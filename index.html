Do You Need a Permit?

Are you a homeowner in California, thinking about starting a home project? Start by checking if you need a permit. This Permit Checker Tool simplifies the process by giving you quick and reliable guidance. Simply select a category, choose your project type, and answer a follow-up question to determine if a permit is required.

Keep in mind that this tool provides a general guideline. Always check with your local building department to ensure compliance with city-specific regulations.
